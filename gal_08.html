Master of Science in Biomedical Communications
Visualizing the Motor Movements of Autism Using Motion Capture Animation to Facilitate Differential Diagnosis
Gwun-Yee Chin, 2010

This MRP used the Unity 3D game development tool to create an application in which autism researchers could view and compare 3D motion capture animations of patients. This project is the first to use motion capture (MoCap) animation to study the movements of ASD in an attempt to facilitate controlled and consistent comparison of movements over large sample populations.

AMI award of Excellence , 2010
