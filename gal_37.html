Antibody-Affinity Maturation by Hypermutation and Selection
Julian Kirk-Elleker, 2007

A demonstration of the micro-evolutionary process of B-cell lineage specialization. This animation and simulation demonstrates how hypermutation within a B-cell population and antigen-affinity selective pressure allows the immune system to respond to novel threats.

AMI Award of Excellence - Interactive/Animation, 2007
